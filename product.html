Local Craft

The beers next door. Nothing says hometown pride like serving the brews that were made just down the street. Brewed with your clientele in mind, our portfolio of local craft beers are diverse, delicious and reflect the local flavors that help your customers feel at home.

  • Aardwolf Brewing Company

    Jacksonville, FL

    Christened after a small mammal whose name means “earth wolf” (which is also the Latin translation of the word hops), Aardwolf Brewing is a highly creative, small-batch brewery with a huge local presence and a keen sense of humor. Their long list of brews include the sessionable Nonchalant America IPA, the Styrofoam Pony American Stout, and the cherry-lime-hibiscus Gose, the Hipster Popsicle – amongst many, many others.

  • Atlantic Beach Brewing Company

    Jacksonville, FL

    Welcome to Atlantic Beach Brewing Company. We are a family-owned brewery whose commitment is simple…pursue our passion for quality craft beer at the beach to please casual drinkers and beer enthusiasts alike. Here’s a little insight into our family, now that we’re sharing!

  • Engine 15 Ciders and Meads

    Jacksonville, FL

    Jacksonville-based convivial gastropub offering a wide selection of beers and hearty bar bites, plus brewing classes.

  • Intuition Ale Works

    Jacksonville, FL

    Established in 2010, this Jacksonville-based craft brewery specializes in handcrafted small-batch ales in a wide range of styles. Florida’s first craft brewery to can their beers, Intuition Ale Works’ flagship is People’s Pale Ale, an American-style pale ale inspired by the west coast. It features two-row malted barley and two caramel malts, magnum hops for bittering, and traditional Williamette and Cascade hops for crisp, citrus aromas. And that’s just the start of Intuition’s lengthy lineup.

  • Pinglehead Brewing Company

    Jacksonville, FL

    Self-described as “beer with attitude,” the people behind Pinglehead Brewing Company practiced their craft from years inside the walls of Orange Park’s Brewer’s Pizza, testing batch after batch with the pizzeria’s willing customers. Now, the brewery produces dozens of handcrafted beers, with six on their permanent roster. Notables include their flagship Pinglehead American Amber featuring a clear red body, off-white head and a highly drinkable bittersweet taste.

  • Ruby Beach Brewing Company

    Jacksonville, FL

    Highlighting the rich history of Jacksonville’s beaches, Ruby Beach Brewing company aims to improve the Jacksonville Beach community by providing a new take on the craft beer movement. Ruby Beach Wheat, American Garage IPA and Twin Finn Lager are just a few of their delicious creations.

  • Southern Swells Brewing Company

    Jacksonville, FL

    As avid homebrewers for the better part of a decade, the decision was made to share a passion for craft beer with Jacksonville. Southern Swells is a place where anyone can relax and sip on delicious craft beers while conversing and sharing a laugh with one another. Southern Swells goal is to brew excellent beer while delivering an equally exceptional taproom experience. Cheers!

  • Veteran’s United Craft Brewery

    Jacksonville, FL

    Founded by a Naval Flight Officer and avid home brewer, Veteran’s United Craft Brewery boasts a focus on high-quality ingredients for Jacksonville’s discerning beer drinkers. Offering three year-round beers, four limited-release brands and a seasonal favorite – amongst multiple small batches – Veteran’s has quickly become a local staple. Year round options include Raging Blonde Ale, a thirst quenching balanced ale with generous late kettle additions of Cascade and Centennial hops. The Hopbanshee India Pale Ale whose “screaming” hop presence inspired the name, and the rich, caramel Scout Dog 44 Amber Ale.

Regional
Craft

The story often starts out the same – beer enthusiasts with a clear vision and an intense desire to try their hand at the art of brewing. And yet, the brews are as different, as diverse and as creative as they come. Our lineup of local craft brewers reflect the southeast corner of our country with artful, thoughtful brews that make your customers feel at home.

  • Coppertail Brewing Company

    Tampa, FL

    Coppertail Brewing Co is an independently owned production brewery and tasting room in the Ybor neighborhood of Tampa, FL. It was founded on the desire to bring more quality, Florida-brewed beer to the thirsty masses. Florida Inspired. Tampa Brewed.

  • Green Man Brewery

    Asheville, FL

    Brewing award-winning ales since 1997, Green Man was founded in downtown Asheville, originally as a brew-pub. In 2010, new ownership and a fresh approach spurred evolutionary change at Green Man.

  • Orange Blossom Brewing Company

    Orlando, FL

    President and founder of Orange Blossom Brewing started brewing in his garage at age 18, launching a lifelong passion for pushing the boundaries of beer. Since 2005, Orange Blossom Pilsner, the flagship beer, has commanded an impressive local draw because of its sessionable, fruity and refreshing taste. The honey beer made with local orange blossom honey reflects the company’s respect for traditional recipes, as well as their curiosity with non-traditional ingredients.

  • Saltwater Brewery

    Delray Beach, FL

    Founded in December 2013 by a team of local Floridians, Saltwater Brewery is Delray Beach's first local production microbrewery, with tasting room and beer garden on sight.

  • Tampa Bay Brewing Company

    Tampa, FL

    Founded in 1995 at the tail end of the last Micro-Brew boom, Tampa Bay Brewing Company’s philosophy is simple: “Brew beer we love to drink!” Tampa Bay produces core beers such as Reef Donkey American Pale Ale and Old Elephant Foot IPA. TBBC operates a Brewpub in Ybor City and a production brewery in northern Tampa.

  • Terrapin Beer Co.

    Athens, GA

    This Athens, Georgia brewery was established by two beer and Grateful Dead enthusiasts, who eventually named their craft brew business after a Grateful Dead song, Terrapin Station. In October of 2002, their Rye Pale Ale was awarded the American Pale Ale Gold Medal at the Great American Beer Festival in Denver, which launched their impending success. In addition to the Rye Pale Ale, their year-round brews include the HI-5 IPA, RecreationalAle, Hopsecutioner IPA and Golden Ale. Four seasonals round out the portfolio.

  • Walking Tree Brewery

    Vero Beach, FL

    Based in Vero Beach, Florida, Walking Tree Brewery combines creativity and science to produce a large selection of unique craft beer. In addition to creating great beer, Walking Tree has developed a culture that unites their community. With heavy importance placed on their Florida roots, the Mangrove tree is a symbol of the Walking Tree name. Mangrove trees live with one foot on land and one in the sea, constantly expanding their boundaries. Through the pursuit to produce unforgettable beer, like the Mangrove tree, Walking Tree Brewery will never forsake their home, their community or their roots.
