The Champion Culture

At Champion Brands, our team of employees is undoubtedly our greatest asset – and we pride ourselves on having a diverse team of hard-working, passionate individuals. Training and team-building are paramount at Champion Brands. Our leadership team is continually working alongside our employees, giving each person the unique opportunity to learn, develop and eventually climb the ranks. Additionally, we recognize that work-life balance is a critical component of both personal and professional success.

Benefits

  • Vacation
  • Paid time off
  • Medical
  • Dental
  • 401K
  • Short-term disability
  • Life insurance
  • Accidental death coverage
  • Training opportunities

In addition to our competitive benefits package, Champion Brands employees enjoy a team-focused, supportive environment where learning, leadership and trust come first.

Delivery Driver

Georgia

Date Posted

We are looking for experienced CDL Delivery Drivers to deliver products from our Distribution Center to the retail outlets, accessing orders from the load on the truck, loading the individual orders onto the hand- truck, and transferring the order into the retailer's cooler, and unloading the order.

Responsibilities

  • Deliver Champion Brands products to stores within in the assigned area.
  • Greet and maintain a professional relationship with customer while providing excellent customer service.
  • Obtain copies of orders to be delivered.
  • Operated hand-truck in a safe manner with order into cooler.

Qualifications

  • High School Diploma or GED required
  • 21 years of age or older
  • Valid Commercial Driver's License (CDL) and driver’s license
  • Ability to lift, and carry up to 50 pounds or more
  • Ability to push and pull several 100 pounds on a hand and/or platform truck
  • Able to work flexible work schedule
