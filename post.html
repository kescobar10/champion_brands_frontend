A day in the life of a Champion beverage

Feb. 28, 2018

At Champion Brands, our truck drivers transport our beverages on a daily basis, and their role is more than merely “throwing cases.” In a given day, they are not only the vessel for delivering our suppliers’ products to bars, restaurants and retail stores, but they are relationship builders, problem-solvers, and mathematicians that are crucial to the continued success of our company—and they’re being all of these things while under extremely tight deadlines and juggling an ever-changing schedule that is beyond their control.

The delivery aspect of our Operations team is truly one of the most important pieces of our company and we want to immerse you in this part of our business so you can learn more about what it’s like to be on the front lines of one of the biggest wholesalers in the state.

A day as part of the operations team at Champion Brands can be a meaningful experience. As a retailer, it’s helpful to understand the nuances of the operations flow.

What stands out to us is our drivers’ ability to adjust on the fly. There are so many curveballs that are thrown at truck drivers over the course of a given day, and it’s up to them to handle the action in an efficient and thoughtful way. Our truck drivers, are key ambassadors between Champion Brands and the places where consumers enjoy their favorite drinks. And their hard work and successes are appreciated by the very people we serve—the customers.

So next time you’re sitting at your favorite bar, just take a moment to realize that the beer you’re sipping on is from a bottle that one of our drivers may have confidently delivered.
